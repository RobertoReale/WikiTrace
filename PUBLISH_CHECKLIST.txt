1. PRIVACY POLICY URL
   - The store requires a public URL because the extension uses chrome.storage.local.
   - Quickest option: create a GitHub Pages site or a Notion/Google Doc published as web page.
   - Content needed (one page is enough):
       "WikiTrace stores your Wikipedia browsing history locally in your browser
        using chrome.storage.local. No data is ever sent to any server, sold, or
        shared. You can delete all data at any time via the Clear All button."
   - After publishing the URL, paste it in the Chrome Web Store Developer Dashboard
     under Privacy > Privacy Policy URL.

2. ICON QUALITY CHECK
   - Open icons/icon16.png, icon48.png, icon128.png in any image viewer.
   - The "W" must be readable. If the font looks bad at 16px, redesign in a tool
     like Figma, Canva, or GIMP and re-export. The generated PNGs are functional
     placeholders — replace them with polished artwork before publishing.

3. DEVELOPER ACCOUNT FEE
   - One-time $5 USD registration at https://chrome.google.com/webstore/devconsole
   - Pay with a credit/debit card tied to a Google account.
